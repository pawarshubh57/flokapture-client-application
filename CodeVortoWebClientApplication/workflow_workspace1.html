Process Payroll Check Amount


This is the rule to initiate payroll processing. It uses employee register and daily ledger to arrive at the daily hours worked and uses leaves calculator module to access the total hours worked to generate the pay amount.

Rule Annotations:

For all employees in the organization
Retrieve employee leaves from leave ledger
Retrieve permitted hours of work for current month
Calculate wages based on hourly rate allowed
Return current pay amount for current month
